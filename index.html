About Us


Founded by Indramani Maurya in 2021, Smart Solution Services deals with all types of Hydraulic parts for sales and services as a channel partner of Wipro which is the biggest hydraulic parts manufacturer in India. After completing B-Tech in 2010 from Kota Technical University, Rajasthan and gaining over 7 years of experience at ATS Industrial Experience, Indore from being a trainee to Assistant Manager, the company is an amalgamation of the experiences gained on field working with different engineers and finding several gaps in the market. Our USP has always been quick response (within 24 hours) team to the services required by our clients.

Our Services


The company currently deals with services such as installation of new hydraulic cylinders in tippers, tip trailers, under body tippers, providing regular maintenance of hydraulic parts such as filter replacements, oil filtration, etc. The work also includes repair of hydraulic cylinders, pump, PTO and control valves. Apart from repairing and installation, the company also provides all types of maintenance kits from hydraulic seal kit to stages of cylinder for both B2C and B2B sectors. Our B2C segment involves working with our clients directly in resolving their issues related to any kind of repairs and installation who are often from and developing customer relationships that eventually leads to business proposition in our B2B sector. In our B2B segment, we work directly with companies (currently only territorial) such as TATA Commercial Waidhan, Hindustan Automobiles, Mamta Motors among many. The segment currently takes about 60% of our total services
